Honduras

Honduras is a vibrant Central American country known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and warm hospitality. It boasts stunning Caribbean beaches, lush rainforests, and ancient Mayan ruins like Copán, making it a destination full of natural beauty and history. Despite facing social and economic challenges, Honduras is home to resilient and hardworking people, whose traditions, cuisine, and music reflect a unique blend of indigenous, African, and Spanish influences.
